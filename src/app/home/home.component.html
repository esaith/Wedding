Joni's
34 S Etna Avenue
Montauk, NY
We understand that your pilates and juice routine might not jive with a full weekend of burger & booze binging, so swing by Joni's for your sprouts fix. Open from early morning until mid-afternoon, this cute place serves eggs, veggies and yummy sandwiches. The Thai Me Up wrap is what we come for, but it's hard to go wrong.

Montauk, NY
For us, Montauk is synonymous with shellfish. Lobster, in particular. And Navy Beach serves theirs right - warm on a buttered bun. No shirt, no shorts, no problem? they don't mind if you stay in your bikini. You're on an island in June, so take full advantage.

Montauk, NY
