The Wynn Hotel Info
  • Rate (Promo) Code: WYNNWED
  • This code cannot be used online.
  • You must call Small Groups Departments to use rate code at 702-777-2223 between 7:30am-6pm(PST), 10:30am-9pm(EST).
  • Provide the staff with "Colon-Lopez and Vanidestine", the WYNNWED rate code and the dates you will be staying.
  • A deposit will be required at the time of confirming the reservation (the first night + tax).

Disclaimer
Please be aware that since this is a holiday weekend, there may be 'wellness checks' by hotel staff or police at any time during your stay. You may want to inquire about this prior to booking. Memorial Day weekend is one of the busiest holidays, and hotels are on high alert for illegal activity, overconsumption of alcohol and drugs, or too much tomfoolery. Additionally, it's worth noting that extended wait times for check-in are not uncommon (like, ridiculously long wait times).
